I am currently a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) with the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Ningbo University, Ningbo, China. I received my BSc in Mechatronics Engineering from Zhejiang University, China in 2017, and MSc in Control Systems Technology from Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e), the Netherlands in 2018. In 2023, I obtained my PhD from TU/e, advised by Gijs Dubbelman and Peter de With.
